Our Gift

This outstanding alternative library is made available free of charge, but the hosting and hard work involved must be covered by somebody. As the library gains popularity, the related expenses increase.

Please Donate Today!

Please contribute a donation. If you published a dynamic cross-browser, cross-DOM browser scripting library, we would donate to you!

Note if you wish to donate, but do not wish to use Pledgie, please contact the author.

Sponsor Recognition

There are four sponsorship levels, each with its own recognition benefits.

Platinum
The Platinum level requires a donation of at least $100 (US). Contributors at this level receive all the benefits of the Gold Level plus a banner ad on the site for a full month!
Gold
The Gold level requires a donation of at least $25 (US). Contributors at this level receive an entry on the Sponsor List, bold and highlighted, with an optional link to your site!
Silver
The Silver level requires a donation of at least $10 (US). Contributors at this level receive an entry on the Sponsor List in bold.
Bronze
The Bronze level requires a donation of at least $5 (US). Contributors at this level receive an entry on the Sponsor List.
